Introduction

Welcome to our analysis of 311 service calls and aldermanic expenditures in Chicago from 2019 to 2023. Our project aims to create an insightful data visualization by integrating public Chicago Data Portal datasets—accessed via API—with web-scraped data from Wikipedia on aldermen and their tenure, alongside expenditures from the 'Menu' program, tabulated by ward. By incorporating GIS ward boundaries, we seek to map and analyze trends in community service requests and budget allocations, offering a comprehensive view of urban management and district-level priorities.
