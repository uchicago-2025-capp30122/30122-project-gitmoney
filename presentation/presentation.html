Introduction

Welcome to our analysis of Chicago’s 311 service calls and aldermanic expenditures from 2019 to 2023. We’ve combined public datasets from the Chicago Data Portal (via API) with web-scraped alderman data from Wikipedia and Menu program expenditures, mapped with GIS ward boundaries.
